Adventures

Kerala's natural beauty and diverse landscapes make it an adventure lover's paradise. Whether you seek the thrill of water sports, the challenge of trekking, or the excitement of wildlife encounters, Kerala has something to offer for every adventure seeker.


Surfing

Ride the waves and embrace the sea with our exciting surfing adventures along the Kerala coast.Here are some of the famous places for surfing in Kerala.

  • Thiruvananthapuram : Kolavam Beach(1K - 3K for training)
  • Varkala : Papanasam Beach (1K - 3K for training)
  • Kochi :Cherai Beach(1K -3K for training)

Ziplining

Experience the adrenaline rush as you zoom through the lush green landscapes on a zipline.Ride solo or in tandem, safely harnessed to a pair overhead cables and pulleys.

  • Karlad Lake in Thariode, Wayanad (INR 250 )
  • Munnar (INR 299)
  • Pattumala Tea Estate in Thekkady(INR 400)

Kayaking

Discover the tranquil beauty of nature while kayaking through the backwater of Kerala. This is an adventure watersport that lets you explore some hidden lagoons and remote canals.
