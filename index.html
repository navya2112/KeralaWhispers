Explore the Enchanting Beauty of Kerala

Welcome to God's Own Country - Kerala , a mesmerizing land of lush greenery, tranquil backwaters, pristine beaches, and a rich cultural heritage. Situated in the southern part of India, Kerala is a heaven for travelers seeking natural beauty and unique experiences.

Uniqueness of Kerala

  • Anamudi Peak , the highest peak in India outside the Himalayas also called the Everest of South India is located in the Ernakulam district of the of Kerala.
  • The Athirappilly Falls , which is situated on the background of Western Ghat mountain ranges, is also known as The Niagara of India.
  • The country's longest lake Vembanad, dominates the backwaters; it lies between Alappuzha and Kochi and is about 200 km2 (77 sq mi) in area.
  • Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple known for finest specimens of Dravidian architecture is located in Thiruvananthapuram, capital city of Kerala.
