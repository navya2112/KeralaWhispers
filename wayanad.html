Wayanad

Wayanad is any nature lover’s paradise.Wayanad is a treasure trove of natural wonders,allowing visitors to immerse themselves in the unparalleled beauty and serenity. It is one of the best travel destinations for nature lovers, adventure seekers as Wayanad proudly glitters its gems – highest peaks, scenic lakes, adventurous sanctuaries, fragrant plantations, lush green paddy fields, graceful waterfalls, and breathtaking views.

Chembra Peak


Surrounded by hills, tea plantations and lush green valley, Chembra Peak is the highest mountain peak in Wayanad. It is 2100 m above sea level. Walking through the tea plantations, towards the mist-covered views, you’ll have amazing experience during trekking and reach a heart shaped lake.

The trek uphill: The total length of the trek is around 4.5 km to the Chembra Peak.You will need permission from the forest department to begin your trek. And only 200 people are given Chembra Peak permission per day, on a first-come-first-serve basis.

The heart-shaped pond:The lake is bound to capture your heart at first sight.After 2-2.5 hours of treking, your eyes will well up at the spectacular sight of the a tiny, heart-shaped lake.

Best time to visit is during August-May.
Visiting Time: 7.00 AM to 5.00PM




Lakkidi View Point


Lakkidi View Point is a tourist destination often referred to as the “Gateway of Wayanad.” It’s known for its mesmerising bird’s eye view of Wayanad and is known for its nine hairpin caves and offers a resplendent view of the mountains in Wayanad. The whole trip of Lakkidi View Point promises an unforgettable charm of natural beauty that should add to their bucket list of attractions in Wayanad.

The complete travel from base to the Lakkidi View Point takes you through the different curves of the mountain road as it is known for & the valley view from the view point is covered with dense vegetation. A stay Vythiri is suggested if you want to enjoy early morning fog.

Lakkidi View Point is about 20 km from Wayanad, on the way to Kozhikode.

Best Time to visit is- November to February
Visiting Time:Early Morning and early Evening




Soochipara Waterfalls


Soochipara Waterfalls (also known as Sentinel Rock Falls) is a breathtaking natural wonder amidst the lush green forests near Wayanad. It’s a must-visit destination for anyone seeking to experience Wayanad’s natural beauty in all its glory.It’s also one of the most photogenic spots for tourists in Wayanad.

It’s 35 km away from Wayanad, surrounded by dense green forests. A 2-km-long, descending trekking trail from the entrance takes you to the falls.Soochipara Waterfalls in Wayanad are surrounded by lush green forests and rocky terrain, making it an ideal trekking spot.

Another enjoyable Soochipara Waterfalls adventure activity is rock climbing. The fall is about 200 meters high, and surrounding it are steep rocks, making it an ideal spot to grab and climb it.

Best Time to visit:October to February
Visiting Time:9 AM to 5 PM
